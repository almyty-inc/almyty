# QA Tour Findings — 2026-06-03 staging

Browser walkthrough of `app.staging.almyty.com` against a freshly registered
account (`qa-tour-1780476584@almyty.test` / org `QA Tour Org`). Five issues
found, ordered by severity.

## 1. Sign-up: ToS checkbox unchecked at form load, submit fails silently

**Severity:** medium — blocks first-time sign-up until the user scrolls to and clicks the checkbox.

The a11y tree reports the "I agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy"
checkbox as `aria-checked=on` at first render, but visually it is unchecked.
Clicking "Create account" does nothing — no validation error, no inline
hint, the button just stays in its disabled-looking state.

Screenshot: `signup-tos-unchecked.png` (Create account button greyed, checkbox empty).

Repro: open `/register`, fill the form, click "Create account" without
explicitly clicking the ToS checkbox.

Fix candidates:
1. Make the disabled-button state visually distinct (it currently looks active because of the violet background).
2. Add an inline validation error when submit is attempted with the checkbox unchecked.
3. Fix the a11y mismatch (`aria-checked` should match the actual state).

## 2. Sidebar: two items highlighted at once on every non-Dashboard page

**Severity:** medium — confusing navigation state on every page in the app.

After navigating away from Dashboard, the sidebar continues to highlight
"Dashboard" in its active-state purple background AND additionally
highlights the current page. So the user sees two purple-background items
simultaneously — Dashboard plus whichever page they actually opened.

Confirmed on: APIs, Gateways, Runners, Memory, Settings (every page hit during the tour).

Screenshots:
- `apis-sidebar-dual-highlight.png`
- `runners-sidebar-dual-highlight.png`

Root cause is likely the sidebar active-link logic mis-firing on initial mount and not clearing.

## 3. Gateways header: "1 gateways (1 active)"

**Severity:** low — typography polish.

On `/gateways`, header reads `1 gateways (1 active) · 0 tool assignments`.
Should pluralize correctly: `1 gateway`. Same on `/runners` with `0 runners ·
0 online` — the zero case is fine but a count of 1 likely has the same issue
(not verified, no runners present).

Screenshot: `gateways-1-gateways-typo.png`.

## 4. Onboarding "Create a gateway" pre-checked on brand-new org

**Severity:** low — false-positive guidance.

The Dashboard's "Welcome to almyty" four-step onboarding shows `1 of 4
complete` and the "Create a gateway" step is strikethrough/checked even
though the user just registered and has created nothing. The reason is
that org creation auto-provisions the system `almyty` MCP gateway, and the
onboarding hint counts any gateway, including system ones.

Screenshot: `dashboard-onboarding-gateway-precheck.png`.

Fix: only count user-created (non-system) gateways for the onboarding step.

## 5. Settings: Members & Teams tab state desync

**Severity:** low — visual only; content is correct.

On `/settings`, clicking the "Members & Teams" top tab loads the correct
content (Members sub-tab with the org owner row), but the visual active
indicator stays on the "Organization" tab. The tab row reads:
`Organization` (purple underlined) | `Members & Teams` | `Profile` | `Security`
even though the content shown is from `Members & Teams`.

Screenshot: `settings-members-teams-tab-desync.png`.
